mjcide was developed as a final project for Compiler class at UFABC (Universidade Federal do ABC).

You can download the project at http://sourceforge.net/projects/mjcide/

The project has two components: a microjava compiler and an IDE.

The microjava compiler is licensed under the MIT license.
The IDE (MIDE) is licensed under the LGPL 2.1 license.
